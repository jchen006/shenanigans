Clover club
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/clover_club_20100
Wayne Collins
Ingredients 
1 free-range egg white, preferably organic
50ml/2fl oz gin
1 tbsp raspberry liqueur 
4 fresh raspberries, plus extra to garnish
25ml/1fl oz freshly squeezed lemon juice
2 tsp sugar syrup
Instructions 
Place the egg white, gin, raspberry liqueur, raspberries, lemon juice and sugar syrup into a cocktail shaker.
Add a handful of ice, shake well and strain the cocktail into a coupette glass. Garnish with a fresh raspberry.
