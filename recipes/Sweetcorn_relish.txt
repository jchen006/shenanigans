Sweetcorn relish
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/sweetcorn_relish_14809
Alex Lomax and Ed Bond
Ingredients 
dash oil
1 onion, chopped
1 garlic clove
2 tsp grated ginger
1-2 chillies, chopped
12 red pepper, chopped
12 green pepper, chopped
2 cobs of sweetcorns, kernels removed
1 large tomato, seeds removed, skinned
2 tbsp dark brown sugar
2 tbsp cider vinegar
12 tsp salt
pepper, to taste
3 tbsp chopped coriander
Instructions 
Heat the oil in a pan and cook the onion, garlic and ginger until soft.
Add the other vegetables to the pan and cook, allowing any liquid to evaporate.
Add the remaining ingredients, except the coriander. Cook for five minutes.
Add the coriander and cook for another minute. Transfer to a sterilised jar and leave to cool.
