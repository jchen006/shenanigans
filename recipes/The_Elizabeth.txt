The Elizabeth
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/theelizabeth_86502
Andy Pearson
Ingredients 
4 gooseberries 
2 tsp white sugar
50ml/2fl oz gin 
25ml/1fl oz elderflower cordial
1 tbsp lime juice 
traditional cloudy lemonade, to top up 
gooseberries and fresh elderflowers, to garnish
Instructions 
In a Boston or whisky glass, mash (muddle) the gooseberries with the sugar using the end of a clean rolling pin.
Add the gin, elderflower cordial and lime juice. Shake well, then strain into a  highball glass filled with ice. Top up with lemonade and garnish with some fresh gooseberries and elderflowers.
