Instructions 
Muddle (crush) the strawberries in a cocktail shaker with a long-handled spoon.
Add the raspberry vodka, strawberry liqueur and lemon sorbet. Add a handful of ice and shake well.
Fill a champagne flute two-thirds full with sparkling white wine and strain the cocktail mixture on top.
Garnish with the whole strawberry.
