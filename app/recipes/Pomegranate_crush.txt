Pomegranate crush
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/_pomegranate_crush_52049
Wayne Collins
Ingredients 
1 tbsp fresh pomegranate seeds, plus extra to garnish
1 tbsp clementine juice
2fl oz vanilla vodka
1fl oz pomegranate juice
fresh mint sprig, to garnish
Instructions 
Place the pomegranate seeds, clementine juice, vanilla vodka and pomegranate juice into a cocktail shaker.  Add a handful of ice and shake well.
Strain the cocktail into a glass filled with ice. Garnish with the mint sprig and extra pomegranate seeds.
