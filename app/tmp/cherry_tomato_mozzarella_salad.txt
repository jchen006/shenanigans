Cherry tomato and mozzarella salad
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/cherry_tomato_and_74745
Mary Berry
Ingredients 
250g/9oz small ripe tomatoes on the vine
250g/9oz mozzarella pearls
about 8 basil leaves, finely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
salt and pepper
Instructions 
Cut the tomatoes in half and place in a bowl with the remaining ingredients.
Season well with salt and pepper, cover and chill until ready to serve.
