Plum pudding cocktail
http://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/plum_pudding_cocktail_77949
Wayne Collins
Ingredients 
1 plum, quartered, stone removed
2 tbsp cognac 
2 tsp amaretto
1 tbsp plum liqueur 
1 tsp cinnamon syrup
freshly grated nutmeg, to garnish
Instructions 
Place the plum, cognac, amaretto, plum liqueur and cinnamon syrup into a cocktail shaker. Add a handful of ice and shake well.
Strain the cocktail into a coupette glass and garnish with freshly grated nutmeg.
